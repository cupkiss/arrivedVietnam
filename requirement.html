Must: Visa

With the exception of a few European passport holders, such as those from the UK, France, and Germany, most nationalities require a visa to enter Vietnam.Consider yourself fortunate if you are from one of these nations because you can enter Vietnam without a visa for 15 days. South Africans must apply in person at the Vietnam embassy closest to them. It is definitely a challenge, but the reward will be well worth the effort, especially considering that you will get to see one of Asia's most stunning locations!

Travelers can check out (this link) to see if you are required to apply for a Vietnam visa.

Single entry tourist visas start at US$25. This goes up to US$155 for a visa valid for two to five years. Please click on the image below to apply e-visa.


Need: Sim

Where to get SIM card? The easiest and most convenient place to buy a SIM card in Vietnam is at the airport, upon your arrival in the country. You’ll find dozens of kiosks selling SIM cards as soon as you exit the baggage claim area in any of Vietnam’s international airports.


Price: Each mobile carrier sets the price of a SIM card and data plan, so the prices should be roughly comparable. If you want to make sure you're getting a good deal, feel free to check at a few different sales kiosks. Depending on how long you plan to stay in Vietnam and how much mobile data you want, a Vietnamese SIM card and prepaid data package should cost between VND 150,000 and 350,000 (US $6.50 to $15).

Should: Applications

Grab Unfortunately, Grab cars are only available in the major cities of the country; Hanoi, Nha Trang, Da Nang, and Ho Chi Minh City Dalat and Quy Nhon are two cities where the Grab engine is present. Although it is inexpensive, we would only recommend using it to travel within the city and not to another city.

XE CurrencyTransfer, monitor and calculate currency as the need arises. This app may not be totally necessary as you are typically tied into rates the banks charge for services. However, it is useful to have around.

Wifi FinderWith this app, you won't have to fumble around town looking for hot spots or guess whether the next location on your list has WiFi.
